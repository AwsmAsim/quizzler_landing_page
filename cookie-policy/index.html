Cookie Policy

Learn how QuizzlerAI Play uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how QuizzlerAI Play uses cookies and similar technologies in our mobile application and related services to enhance your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use our app or visit our website. They help us provide a personalized experience, improve our services, and understand how users interact with our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: Enable core functionality, such as user authentication and session management.
  • Examples: Login tokens, user preferences.
  • Duration: Session-based or persistent until account deletion.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: Collect data on app usage to improve performance and features.
  • Examples: Firebase Analytics for tracking user interactions.
  • Duration: Up to 12 months, then anonymized.

2.3 Third-Party Cookies

  • Purpose: Support third-party services like Google and Apple authentication.
  • Examples: OAuth tokens for social login.
  • Duration: Varies based on third-party policies.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in and secure your account.
  • Personalization: Remember your quiz preferences and settings.
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to enhance app functionality.
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • App Settings: Opt out of non-essential analytics cookies.
  • Device Settings: Adjust browser or device settings to block cookies (note: this may affect app functionality).
  • Third-Party Opt-Outs: Manage preferences via Google or Apple account settings.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not use cookies to track or target children under 13. Any analytics data collected from children is anonymized and used solely to improve the app's educational features.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted in the app, and continued use constitutes acceptance.
