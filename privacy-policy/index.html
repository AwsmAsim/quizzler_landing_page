Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QuizzlerAI Play collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered multiplayer quiz application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for email login)
  • Profile information from Google or Apple (when using social login)
  • Username and display preferences
  • Authentication tokens for secure access

1.2 Quiz and Gameplay Data

During your use of the app, we collect:

  • Quiz topics, keywords, and settings you specify
  • Questions generated by our AI system
  • Your answers and response times
  • Quiz results and performance statistics
  • Leaderboard positions and rankings
  • Multiplayer game sessions and participant data

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device information and operating system
  • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Network connection data
  • Firebase analytics data for app improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Generate personalized AI quizzes based on your preferences
  • Enable real-time multiplayer quiz sessions
  • Maintain leaderboards and track performance
  • Provide user authentication and account management

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve AI quiz generation
  • Fix bugs and enhance app performance
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored using Firebase, Google's backend-as-a-service platform. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and data minimization practices

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • AI Service Providers: Quiz generation and content processing
  • Authentication Providers: Google and Apple for social login

4.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your quiz data and statistics
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection

6. Children's Privacy

QuizzlerAI Play is designed for users of all ages, including children. We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • We collect minimal information from users under 13
  • Parental consent is required for children under 13
  • Children's quiz data is automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity
  • No advertising or marketing targeting children

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adherence to international privacy frameworks
  • Regular compliance audits

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion
  • Quiz Data: Retained for 2 years for leaderboard integrity
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
  • Crash Reports: Automatically deleted after 90 days
